## Executive Summary

The two-day Doha technical round (1–2 Jul) concluded with Qatar and Pakistan mediators reporting "positive progress" on MOU implementation: a communications channel to flag violations was agreed and a goods-purchase mechanism for the first $3 bn frozen-asset tranche was sketched in principle, with no cash transfer authorised. Iranian negotiators departed Doha immediately as Tehran prepares for the six-day Khamenei funeral (4–9 Jul). The corridor sovereignty and toll dispute — and all nuclear agenda items — remain unresolved; the next session will be scheduled "at the earliest possible time" after the funeral. Kinetic stand-down holds into Day 16. The 11th MEU (USS Boxer ARG, ~2,000+ Marines) is now confirmed operating in the Middle East. Mojtaba Khamenei, seriously wounded in the February 28 strike, is unlikely to appear at his father's funeral per his India representative — sustaining leadership-legitimacy uncertainty.
