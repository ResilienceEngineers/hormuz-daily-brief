## Executive Summary

The 26–28 June mutual-strike exchange — US strikes on IRGC targets, IRGC ballistic missiles on Kuwait and Bahrain US bases — brought the ceasefire to its most fragile point since the 17–18 June MOU signing. Round 2 Burgenstock talks were disrupted by the mutual-strike cascade; US envoy Witkoff and Kushner are now heading to Doha for 30 June meetings, while Iran's FM insists no negotiations are scheduled.
