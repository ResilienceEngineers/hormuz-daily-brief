## Executive Summary

After three days of sharp US–Iran kinetic exchange (10–11 Jun), Trump suspended planned third-wave strikes 11 Jun citing a diplomatic breakthrough, with AP/PBS confirming a deal framework had been reached via Pakistan mediation.
